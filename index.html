Model structure

Adoption is modeled as an interacting system, not a static forecast.

01

Charging infrastructure

More EVs increase charger demand. Additional stations reduce waiting time and improve EV convenience after a construction delay.

02

Consumer utility

Adoption depends on purchase price, operating cost, range, charging convenience, and social exposure.

03

Technology learning

EV range, price, and efficiency improve over time, changing the attractiveness of EVs relative to conventional vehicles.

04

Word of mouth

Higher EV share increases consumer exposure to EV ownership, reinforcing future adoption.
